Northeast Ohio Mesh Network Map

What is this map?


This map shows the approximate locations of static Meshtastic nodes submitted by members of the community. The circles indicate approximate location (this is done for privacy) and do not indicate node range or coverage. These details are updated manually and are not necessarily accurate.


Click on any of the circles for additional information.
